OpenNote AI

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

OpenNote AI is a local-first notes, documents, recording, handwriting, and AI assistant app. This policy explains what information the app handles and how that information is protected.

OpenNote AI does not send users' notes, documents, PDFs, recordings, handwriting, transcripts, prompts, or AI conversations to third-party AI services.

Privacy Policy Terms of Use Support

1. Overview

OpenNote AI helps you create and organize notes, text documents, PDFs, handwriting, audio recordings, transcripts, and AI-assisted outputs. The app is designed around local-first processing and user control.

2. Local-First AI Processing

OpenNote AI does not send users' notes, documents, PDFs, recordings, handwriting, transcripts, prompts, or AI conversations to third-party AI services.

AI features are designed to run on-device when available.

If a future version adds optional cloud AI that sends user-selected content to a third-party AI service, the app will clearly explain what data is sent, identify the service provider, and ask for permission before sending that content.

3. Notes, Documents, Recordings, and Transcripts

OpenNote AI may handle:

  • notes and text documents
  • PDFs and imported files
  • handwriting and Apple Pencil content
  • audio recordings
  • transcripts
  • AI-assisted outputs generated inside the app
  • folders, tags, organization data, and preferences

This content is used only for app functionality. It is not sold, not used for advertising, and not sent to third-party AI services.

4. Optional Local AI Model Downloads

Optional local AI model downloads may contact model file hosts such as Hugging Face, ModelScope, GitHub Releases, or another configured file hosting source only to download model files.

Model hosts may receive standard network information such as:

  • IP address
  • request time
  • requested file URL
  • normal device/network metadata included in a download request

OpenNote AI does not send users' notes, documents, PDFs, recordings, handwriting, transcripts, prompts, or AI conversations to model file hosts.

5. Speech Recognition, Transcription, and Translation

OpenNote AI prefers on-device processing where available. If Apple system frameworks are used, processing is handled according to Apple's system privacy protections and device permissions.

6. Sign in with Apple and Account Data

If you sign in with Apple, Apple may provide an app-specific user identifier and, depending on your choice, your name or email address. OpenNote AI uses this information only for account access, sync-related functionality where available, account management, and paid feature access.

7. Subscriptions and Purchases

Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. OpenNote AI may receive subscription status needed to unlock Plus, Pro, or other paid features.

8. iCloud and Sync

If sync is available and enabled, user data may be stored or synced through Apple iCloud or the configured sync service only to provide sync across the user's devices. Synced content is not used for advertising and not sent to third-party AI services.

9. Data Not Sold or Used for Tracking

OpenNote AI does not sell personal information.

OpenNote AI does not use advertising SDKs, cross-app tracking, or third-party tracking pixels.

OpenNote AI does not use users' notes, documents, PDFs, recordings, handwriting, transcripts, prompts, or AI conversations to train third-party AI models.

10. Diagnostics and App Performance

OpenNote AI may receive limited diagnostic or crash information if provided by Apple or platform tools. This information is used only to improve app stability and performance.

11. Account Deletion

Users can delete their OpenNote AI account directly inside the app:

Settings > Account > Delete Account

12. Data Security

OpenNote AI uses platform-provided storage, security, and account features where available. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

13. Children

OpenNote AI is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Updates to this policy will change the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
