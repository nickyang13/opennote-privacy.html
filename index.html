OpenNote

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

OpenNote is designed as a local-first notes, recording, document, and AI assistant app. This Privacy Policy explains what information OpenNote handles and how it is protected.

1. Information We Do Not Sell or Track

OpenNote does not sell your personal information. OpenNote does not use advertising SDKs, cross-app tracking, or third-party tracking pixels.

2. Notes, Documents, Recordings, and Transcripts

Your notes, imported documents, audio recordings, transcripts, translations, and local AI conversations are intended to be stored and processed on your device, unless you choose to use a feature that explicitly requires external services.

OpenNote does not intentionally upload your private note content, recordings, or transcripts to our own servers for advertising or resale.

3. Local AI Models

OpenNote may allow you to download local AI model files, such as Gemma-compatible models, so that AI features can run on your device. When downloading model files, your device may contact model hosting providers or mirrors such as Hugging Face, ModelScope, or other configured download sources.

These download providers may receive standard network information, such as your IP address, request time, and requested file URL. OpenNote does not send your notes or recordings to those download sources as part of model download.

4. Speech Recognition and Translation

OpenNote is built to prefer on-device processing where available. Audio transcription, transcript display, and translation features may depend on Apple system frameworks, local AI models, or features you enable in the app.

5. Sign in with Apple and Subscriptions

If OpenNote offers Sign in with Apple, Apple may provide OpenNote with a user identifier and, depending on your choice, an email address. If OpenNote offers paid subscriptions or in-app purchases, payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. OpenNote may receive purchase status information needed to unlock paid features.

6. iCloud and Sync

If you enable iCloud or other sync features in the future, your content may be stored in Apple iCloud or the selected sync service according to that provider's terms and privacy practices. OpenNote will only use sync functionality to provide the features you choose.

7. Data Security

OpenNote uses platform-provided storage and security features where available. You are responsible for protecting access to your device, Apple ID, backups, and any files you export or share from OpenNote.

8. Children

OpenNote is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through OpenNote, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when OpenNote changes, when new features are added, or when legal requirements change. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
