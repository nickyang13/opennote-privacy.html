Privacy & AI Processing

OpenNote AI is designed to be local-first. Your notes, documents, PDFs, recordings, handwriting, transcripts, prompts, and AI conversations are not sent to third-party AI services.

Account Deletion

Users can delete their OpenNote AI account directly inside the app: Settings > Account > Delete Account.

Subscriptions

Purchases and subscriptions are managed by Apple through the App Store.

Users can manage or cancel subscriptions in Apple ID > Subscriptions.

Users can tap Restore Purchases in the app if subscription access does not appear.

Model Downloads

Optional local AI model downloads may contact model file hosts only to download model files. Model file hosts may receive standard network information such as IP address, request time, and requested file URL.

User notes, PDFs, recordings, handwriting, transcripts, prompts, and AI conversations are not sent to model file hosts.
