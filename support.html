Model Downloads

OpenNote may download local AI model files from configured model hosting sources. Download speed and availability can vary by country, network provider, and hosting provider. If a model download fails, try another network and confirm that your device has enough free storage.

Subscriptions

Purchases and subscriptions are managed by Apple. To manage or cancel a subscription, open the Settings app on your device, tap your Apple ID, then tap Subscriptions.
